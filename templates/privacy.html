Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 22, 2025

At CutStack, accessible from cutstack.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by CutStack and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Uploaded Files: When you use our PDF imposition tool, you upload files to our server for processing. These files are processed strictly in-memory and are not permanentely stored on our disk storage. From the moment the processing is complete and the file is sent back to you, the data is discarded from our system memory.

Log Files: Like many other websites, CutStack makes use of log files. These files merely log visitors to the site. The information collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This is not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

2. Cookies and Web Beacons

CutStack uses 'cookies' to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.
